# Guidance 3.0

Guidance 3.0 is a complete rebuild for modern macOS. The app keeps the simple menu bar workflow of the original Guidance, but adds desktop widgets, a redesigned Settings window, expanded languages, customizable widget themes, and a much more powerful notification and audio system.

## Highlights

- Complete modern SwiftUI rebuild for macOS.
- New desktop and Notification Center widgets in small, medium, and large sizes.
- Widgets show prayer times, countdowns, Hijri date, location, reminders, silent mode, and active audio.
- Stop adhan, dua, or reminder audio from the widget, notification, or menu bar.
- New Appearance settings with Nocturne, Dawn, Oasis, Ink, High Contrast, and custom widget themes.
- Redesigned Settings with clearer General, Location, Calculation, Adjustments, Notifications, and Appearance sections.
- Expanded language support: English, Arabic, French, Urdu, Indonesian, Turkish, Persian, Bengali, and Malay.
- In-app language switching, live localized UI updates, localized digits, and right-to-left layouts for Arabic, Urdu, and Persian.
- Per-prayer notification settings with main alerts, pre-prayer reminders, post-prayer reminders, separate sounds, and custom offsets.
- Jumu'ah override for separate Friday Dhuhr notification settings.
- Built-in adhan sounds, custom audio files, system sound, no sound, volume control, global silent mode, and optional dua after adhan.
- Smarter menu bar states with imminent prayer color, active audio colors, location attention badge, and a 15-minute Iqama count-up.
- Improved location search, manual time zone selection, country-based calculation method detection, and offline reverse-geocode retry.
- Added Singapore and Tehran calculation methods, automatic high-latitude rule detection, and better extreme high-latitude fallback.
- Existing preferences migrate where possible, including calculation settings, notifications, custom sounds, location, Hijri offset, and menu bar display choices.
